## WHAT NOT TO DO

### Mistakes to Avoid

‚ùå **Don't oversell**
- Be honest about risks and unknowns
- Admit what you don't know
- Show humility

‚ùå **Don't rush**
- Let the conversation breathe
- Pause after key points
- Give him time to process

‚ùå **Don't corner him**
- Don't force a decision today
- Respect if he needs time to think
- Make it easy for him to say "not right now"

‚ùå **Don't ignore objections**
- Take concerns seriously
- Don't be defensive
- If he's right about a risk, acknowledge it

‚ùå **Don't talk over him**
- Listen actively
- Ask follow-up questions
- Show you value his input
